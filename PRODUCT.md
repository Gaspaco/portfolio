# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Creative directors, agencies, and potential collaborators browsing on desktop (primarily) and mobile. They're scanning portfolios quickly, looking for craft quality and personality that separates this developer from hundreds of others.

## Product Purpose
Personal portfolio for Niko, a creative developer based in The Netherlands. The site IS the proof of skill: every interaction, animation, and layout choice demonstrates what Niko builds for clients. Success means a visitor remembers this site after closing the tab.

## Brand Personality
Bold, Precise, Cinematic. High-contrast, intentional, every frame composed like a shot. Confidence without arrogance. Technical mastery visible in the details, not shouted.

## Anti-references
- Generic template portfolios (Wix/Squarespace/ThemeForest aesthetic)
- Overly minimal / empty sites that feel like placeholders
- Cookie-cutter developer portfolios with identical card grids and hero patterns

## Design Principles
1. **Every frame is composed** — Treat each viewport state as a deliberate composition, not a responsive accident
2. **Show the craft, don't describe it** — The site's own quality is the portfolio piece; no need to explain what you do when the visitor is experiencing it
3. **Tension creates memory** — Use contrast (scale, color, motion, silence) to create moments that stick
4. **Restraint is confidence** — One strong choice beats three safe ones; let negative space do work

## Accessibility & Inclusion
WCAG 2.1 AA minimum. Respect prefers-reduced-motion. Ensure text contrast ratios meet 4.5:1 for body, 3:1 for large text. Site must be navigable without custom cursor.
